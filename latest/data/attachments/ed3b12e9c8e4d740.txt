We have two great restaurant options right near Copper Fox Gifts!

**Muggs of Mille Lacs** is next door to us and serves some of the best burgers in the lake area, along with a large selection of draft beers and great pub fare.

**Tulibee Tavern** is across the street and serves more home-cooked meals at reasonable prices.

Both are very convenient to our store and great spots to grab a meal before or after shopping!
